Dalgona Coffee

Dalgon coffee is a South Korean fantasy recipe! This dish is very popular on WhatsApp Status, and across the world. It was highly treated as a lockdown activity, nomiating one of the friends to try it.It is pretty much easy to make.

About Chef

Chef Pranshu is the youngest chef. Though he did not try out the recipe personally, he has helped us in understanding what the recipe is!

Ingredients

  • Hot Water
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Coffee Powder
Step 1: Add hot water and coffee powder
Add a cup of hot water into a bowl, add 2 tablespoons of coffe powder and 3 tablespoons of sugar in to it

Step 2: Whisk until you get a fluffy mixture
Use a hand held mixer to whisk the coffe powder, sugar and hot water mixture, till it turns into a fluffy creamy mixture.

Step 3: Serve with milk
Take a cup of hot/cold milk and add the coffee cream on top Serve with spoon and straw. Enjoy your Lockdown recipe!
